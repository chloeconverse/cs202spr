3 of the Best Hiking Destinations

This is a flat, easy walk along a bright blue lake called Lake Louise in Canada. You walk on a wide path with big mountains and a glacier around you. It's perfect for kids and anyone who likes pretty views without climbing.

The Pipiwai Trail is in Hawaii, on the island of Maui. It takes you through a cool bamboo forest, past a big tree, and ends at a tall waterfall. It's not too hard, and the jungle feels like a fun adventure for families.

This is a walk between two colorful villages called Monterosso and Vernazza in Italy. The Cinque Terre Trail has amazing sea views and cute houses on cliffs. It's a bit hilly, but many families enjoy it, and you can take a train back if you get tired.
