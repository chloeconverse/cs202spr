Deserts and Arid Landscapes

3 of the Best Deserts and Arid Landscapes

The Sahara Desert is in North Africa. It is the largest hot desert in the world. It has huge sand dunes, very little water, and very hot days.

The Atacama Desert is in Chile, South America. It is one of the driest places on Earth. Some parts of it get no rain for years. The land is rocky and dry.

The Arabian Desert is in the Middle East. It covers parts of countries like Saudi Arabia and the UAE. It has sand, stone, and high temperatures.
