3 of the Best Jungles and Rainforests

The Amazon Rainforest is in South America. It is the largest rainforest in the world. It has many trees, animals, and lots of rain.

The Congo Rainforest is in Central Africa. It is full of tall trees, rivers, and wild animals. It is the second largest rainforest after the Amazon.

The Daintree Rainforest is in Australia. It is very old and has many plants and animals. It rains a lot, and the forest is very green.
