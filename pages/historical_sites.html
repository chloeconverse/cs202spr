3 of the Best Historical Sites

The Great Wall of China is a long wall built a long time ago to protect China. It is made of stone and bricks and goes across hills and mountains.

The Pyramids of Giza are in Egypt. They were built a long time ago as tombs for pharaohs. They are big and made of stone blocks.

Machu Picchu is an old city in the mountains of Peru. It was built by the Inca people. It has stone buildings and is very high up.
