Major Cities

3 of the Best Cities

New York City is in the USA. It is a big and busy city. You can see tall buildings like the Empire State Building, visit Central Park, and see the Statue of Liberty.

Tokyo is in Japan. It is a modern city with fast trains, bright lights, and many people. You can eat sushi, visit temples, and see Mount Fuji from far away.

Paris is in France. It is known for its art, food, and fashion. You can see the Eiffel Tower, visit museums like the Louvre, and eat bread and cheese.
