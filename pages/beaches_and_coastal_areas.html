Beaches and Coastal Areas

3 of the Best Cities

Bondi Beach is in Sydney, Australia. It is famous for its golden sand and big waves. Many people go there to surf, swim, or relax in the sun.

Copacabana Beach is in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It is a long beach with soft sand and clear water. People enjoy playing sports, dancing, and having fun there.

Waikiki Beach is in Honolulu, Hawaii. It has beautiful blue water and is great for learning to surf. Many tourists visit this beach to enjoy the warm weather and ocean.
